Commercial Use Articles in JFP

The Journal of Functional Programming (JFP) has recently added a new kind of article: a Commercial Uses Article. Articles in this series focus on the use of functional languages as a means not an end. The JFP web site gives more detailed information about Commercial Use Articles.

Members of the CUFP community are encouraged to submit papers to the series, describing their uses of functional languages. Questions may be directed to Kathleen Fisher, who edits the series.
